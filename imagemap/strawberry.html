Strawberries

The strawberry or Fragaria x ananassa for the technical of you is a bush (middle) that grows the flowers (right) that turn into these deliciously adorable and versitile little fruits we call strawberries (left). It is used in sweeteners, juices, baking, aromas, candy, lip gloss or just being eaten raw.

Strawberry cobbler

Step 1:Clean and cut 4 cups of strawberries

Step 2:Preheat oven to 375oF and evenly spread strawberries in a 8 or 9 in. square baking dish.

Step 3:In a medium mixing bowl mix together, 1cup sugar and all purpose flour and 1/2Tsp. of baking powder.

Step 3:Add 1 beaten egg to the mixture and mix until crumbly and everything is incorperated.

Step 4:Spread over the berries.

Step 5:Dot with little bits of butter no more than 1/4cups worth should be neccessary.

Step 6:Bake for 45-50 minutes.

Note:It should be cooled a bit before serving.
