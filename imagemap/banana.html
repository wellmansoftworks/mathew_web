The Musa acuminata x M. balbisiana, or the modern banana tree (above) usualy stands around 5 ft. tall and once matured will produce flowers (right) that will turn into bananas (left).

Banana smoothie

How to make: Its vary simple all you need to to do is blend the following: 2 bananas; broken into chunks, 2 cups of milk, 1/2 cups of peanut butter, 2 Tbsp of honey and 2 cups of ice cubes. Blend for about 30 seconds.
