Apples

The apple tree or Malus pumila is the wonderfull tree (middle) responable for these delicious things we know and love called apples (left). It is a deciduous tree that blossums in the spring and stands anywhere from around 6 - 30ft. These blossums (Right) are what turn into apples come late summer to early autumn.

Spooky apple mouths

Step 1:Core an apple.

Step 2:cut it into fours.

Step 3:cut a deep slit in the peices to make a mouth.

Step 4:Fill the slit with peanut butter and put almond slices for the teeth.
