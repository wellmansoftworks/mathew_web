Terraria is a 2-D sandbox crafting / survival game where the main objective is to continueously get better gear to slay stronger bosses


What I really like about the game mainly is the sheer diversity of items and things to do.
There are nearly 3000+ items in the game currently (Almost half of which are weapons and armour) and there are (in the PC version) 13 bosses to fight, each one having there own attack patterns and unique drops.
