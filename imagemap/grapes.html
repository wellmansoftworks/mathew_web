Grapes

The Vitis vinifera or the common grape vine (middle) is highly versitile and wide spread, having been cultivated everywhere in the world exept Antarctica. They are versitile because they are used to make many things like wines, juices, medicine, baking, raisens or just being eaten raw.

Concord Grape Jelly

Step 1:Clean and destem 3 1/2 lbs. of concord grapes then place into large kettle and crush.

Step 2:Add 1/2 of water, cover and bring to a boil over high heat.

Step 3:Turn heat down to low and let simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 4:Take off of heat and extract juice then chill overnight.

Step 5:Strain juice through 2 damp cheese cloths.

Step 6:Measure 4 cups of the strained juice and stir in 7 cups of sugar in a pot.

Step 7:Quickly bring to a rolling boil. Stir in 3 Fld. ounces of liquid pectin and let boil for 1 minute then remove from heat and skim off foam.

Step 8:Pour into hot, sterile jars and process in boiling water bath for 5 minutes.
